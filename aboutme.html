I am a Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo.

I hack together all types of applications, from production web applications (PiinPoint, Hack the North), to published mobile apps (PiinPoint). A lot of motivation behind what I make is to learn new things, such as creating a Z80 emulator and an operating system.

In my free time I also enjoy photography, star gazing, urban exploration, participating in hackathons, and calling myself a game guru.

I have a strong passion for what I study, and love solving problems using what I've learned! Among my plans is further studies in a spread of cryptography, hardware architecture, network systems, compilers, operating systems, and so much more!

If you want to learn more about my ventures and learnings, check out my Medium.
